    About me

    SPORTS

    My two favorite sports are running and basketball. I have ran for most of my life, starting with track and field at the
    age of 5. My friends call me crazy for loving to run but I find it crazy they don't feel the same way. In highschool,
    I've done cross country my freshman and senior years, and track all four years. Starting in 7th grade, I joined a group called
    Students Run LA (SRLA), and have ran the LA Marathon 5 times. I joined the Basketball team my sophmore year and worked my
    way to being named JV Captain. Junior year I got moved up to Varsity and kept my title of captain. Junior year, for the first time in
    Canoga Park High School history our boys Basketball team won the City Championship! This year I was the head captain and I
    enjoyed every last minute of my basketball career.



    BAND

    Band has been one of my favorite experiences of my high school career. I've played the trumpet for eight years.
    When I first joined the Canoga Park Band we had 16 to 18 people. Over the years our program has grown and we now stand
    at a strong 40. I played Trumpet freshman through junior year and this year held the honor of Drum Major. This past year I
    also made Drummajor for the LAUSD All-City Marching band. My sophmore year I had the opportunity to be a part of the LAUSD
    ALL-City Marching Band. We march several parades including the Rose Parade. Near the end of our season I received and honorable
    mention for my hard work. I joined the All-City band the following year and was awarded the 2017-2018 Marching King.



    SCHOOL

    Right now my favorite class is physics. I love problem solving and that is one of the main reasons I am persuing the STEM
    field and engineering. My teacher is very funny and makes learning physics more fun. At this time I lean more toward persuing
    Mechanical Engineering but still want to explore my options and see what the other fields are like.
