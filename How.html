    THE GOAL

    The goal is to have a microphone attached to the end of my trumpet. The Pi will save the audio and play it back.
    My code transposes the music from B flat to C. Then the notes are put through a spreadsheet inputing the values
    to the musescore mscx. format. Run through Musescore to screat sheet music. Print sheet music.
