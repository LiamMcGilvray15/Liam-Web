    Basketball


    This was the final competition. The goal of this challange was to score as many point possible in the time given. There were three
    rounds, each time the rules were getting more stricked. There were three different kinds of baskets:

    -1 pointers: These were really low, on a moving platform, and the balls would stay once scored.
    -2 pointers: These were medium hight, in two of the corners, and the balls return from the bottom of the basket.
    -3 pointers: These were the tall baskets, in two of the corners, the balls return from the bottom of the basket.

    My design collected the balls throught front of a conveyour belt. There was a small strorage strip for the balls on the back of my
    robot that would lead to the second conveyor belt. The second conveyor belt would drop the ball in the the medium basket. The balls
    would cycle through this system over and over again.
